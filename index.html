Trezor.io/start - Trezor Hardware Wallet (Official)

Welcome to the world of cryptocurrency security! Setting up your Trezor hardware wallet is a crucial step to ensure your digital assets are safe. Follow these instructions carefully to configure your Trezor device properly.

1. Unbox Your Trezor Device

Start by unboxing your Trezor hardware wallet. Inside, you should find the Trezor device itself, a USB cable, and a recovery seed card. Make sure all items are present and in good condition.

2. Connect Your Trezor to Your Computer

Plug the USB cable into the Trezor device and connect the other end to your computer. Visit trezor.io/start to access the official setup page.

3. Access the Setup Page

Once you are on the setup page, select the type of Trezor device you are using—Trezor One or Trezor Model T. The website will guide you through the specific setup steps for your model.

4. Install the Trezor Bridge

To interact with your Trezor wallet, you will need the Trezor Bridge software. Follow the on-screen instructions to download and install this software. Trezor Bridge ensures seamless communication between your Trezor device and your computer.

5. Initialize Your Device

After installing Trezor Bridge, return to the setup page. Click “Initialize Device” and follow the instructions on your Trezor screen. You will be prompted to create a new wallet.

6. Create a PIN

You will need to set a secure PIN for your Trezor device. The PIN protects your device from unauthorized access. Choose a PIN that is both secure and memorable. You will enter this PIN each time you connect your Trezor to a computer.

7. Write Down Your Recovery Seed

The recovery seed is a crucial part of your wallet's security. It’s a series of 24 words generated by your Trezor device. Write these words down on the provided recovery seed card and store it in a safe place. This seed allows you to recover your wallet if your device is lost or damaged.

8. Confirm Your Recovery Seed

To ensure you’ve written down the recovery seed correctly, you will be asked to confirm it. Follow the prompts to enter a few of the words from your recovery seed to verify that you have recorded it accurately.

9. Complete the Setup

Once you’ve confirmed your recovery seed, the setup is complete. Your Trezor device is now ready to use. You can access your wallet through the Trezor web interface or the Trezor Suite application, which provides additional management and security features.

10. Secure Your Trezor

Always keep your Trezor device and recovery seed in secure locations. Avoid sharing your PIN or recovery seed with anyone. Regularly update your Trezor firmware to ensure you have the latest security enhancements.

Conclusion

Setting up your Trezor hardware wallet is a straightforward process, but it's essential to follow each step carefully to ensure your cryptocurrency is protected. For more details or troubleshooting, refer to the official Trezor support resources.
